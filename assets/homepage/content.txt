The AI research team in Dresden (https://www.baua.de/DE/Forschung/KI-Nachwuchsgruppe) focuses on high-risk applications of AI in machinery. 

According to the European Machinery Directive (2006 and 2023) a risk assessment has to be conducted before placing a new machine on the European market. Therefore, the use of ML components in safety-critical systems will depend significantly on the ability to provide comprehensive proof of their safety. Furthermore, the European AI Act imposes strict technical requirements for high-risk AI in safety-critical machinery, e.g. in terms of accuracy and robustness.

We aim to bridge the gap between traditional safety engineering standards and the evolving requirements of ML and AI development, ensuring that these innovative technologies can meet stringent regulatory and safety demands. Through our research, we contribute to setting the foundations for reliable, compliant, and safe AI deployment in industrial environments.
